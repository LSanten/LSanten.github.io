About Myself


Thank you for visiting my website. This "About Myself" section could be summarized as "What I do and think about". It is an exploration of my identity as engineer and artist. You can find a variety of my hobbies and inspirational sources down below.

I will try describe my thoughts on important matters such as climate change, design, and politically conscious engineering with the aid of small essays. All sections below describe parts that define me as an individual and professional.
